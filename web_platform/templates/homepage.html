Data Sources & Processing

We utilized comprehensive NASA Open Science Data Repository (OSDR) datasets, including SpaceX Inspiration4 mission biomarker data, bedrest studies, and longitudinal cardiovascular monitoring data. Our preprocessing pipeline handles missing values, normalizes biomarker levels, and engineers temporal features.

Primary Datasets
  • OSD-258: SpaceX Inspiration4 RNA-seq
  • OSD-484: Cardiac gene expression
  • OSD-575: Comprehensive metabolic panel
Validation Datasets
  • OSD-51: Microarray bedrest studies
  • OSD-635: Bulk RNA-seq validation
  • Published bedrest study benchmarks
Machine Learning Pipeline

Our ensemble approach combines multiple algorithms optimized for different clinical scenarios. The clinical model (ElasticNet) prioritizes interpretability for medical decision-making, while the research ensemble maximizes predictive accuracy.

Model Architecture Details
Clinical Model: ElasticNet (α=0.1, l1_ratio=0.5)
Research Ensemble: Weighted Average of:
├── ElasticNet (weight: 0.4)
├── Random Forest (weight: 0.3)
└── XGBoost (weight: 0.3)

Validation: Time-series CV (5 splits)
Feature Selection: Consensus (K-Best + RFE + RF importance)
